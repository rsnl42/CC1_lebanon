Strategic Insights

  • Lebanon Crisis Profile
    Our analysis shows Lebanon's out-of-school rate peaked at 38.91%, highlighting a severe education gap during regional escalations.
  • The 50km Risk Zone
    Approximately 41.5% of schools in the dataset (21,861 sites) are classified as "Vulnerable," having been within 50km of a conflict event.
  • Gendered Survival Gap
    In high-fatality zones, survival rates to the last grade of primary drop significantly, with girls showing 12% higher volatility than boys.

Recommended Actions

  • Focus on 21k Vulnerable Sites: Target the 41.5% of schools in the risk zone for pre-emptive resource caching and teacher "continuity" training.
  • Lebanon Transition Support: Implement targeted secondary-school scholarships in Lebanon to bridge the 39% OOS gap and prevent entry into informal labor.
  • Dynamic Buffer Zones: Use the Geospatial Timeline to predict the "path of conflict" and establish mobile learning centers 3-6 months ahead of forecasted volatility.
